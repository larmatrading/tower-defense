Tower Defense Game

How to Play:

    Click on an available tower placement location to build a tower.

    Each tower type has its own unique abilities and costs. Choose your towers wisely.

    Defeat waves of enemies to earn coins. Use coins to build and upgrade your towers.

    If enemies breach your defenses, you'll lose health.

Are you ready to defend your territory and conquer the enemy waves? Click "Play" to start the game!
